Structure your resume. Divide it into sections such as "Education", "Work Experience", "Skills", "Achievements", etc.
Use keywords and phrases that match the job you are applying for. This will help your resume get into the list of candidates that will be analyzed by the program.
Describe your work experience and achievements specifically and clearly. Specify what tasks you performed and what results you achieved.
Specify your skills and qualifications that match the requirements of the job. Don't forget to mention your knowledge of programming languages, databases, content management systems, etc.
Specify your personal qualities that can be useful in work, such as communicativeness, responsibility, creative approach, etc
Don't forget to provide contact information such as email and phone number. Make sure they are listed correctly and up-to-date.
Check your resume for errors and typos. This can affect your chances of getting a job.
